Some Information about the weather

Clear weather

Clear weather happens when the sky is cloudless, and sunlight illuminates the earth clearly. This occurs due to clean air and an abundance of sunlight. The temperature can be comfortable and warm, depending on the region and season.

Cloudy weather

Cloudy weather is marked by numerous clouds covering the sky. The clouds can vary in thickness and type. During cloudy weather, sunlight may still be visible, but its brightness is reduced by the clouds. The temperature is generally not too high due to the blocked sunlight.

Rainy weather

Rainy weather occurs when water droplets fall from the clouds to the Earth's surface. Rain is formed when water vapor in the atmosphere condenses into heavy droplets and descends to the ground due to gravity. Rain intensity can vary from drizzle to heavy downpours.

Weather Forecast

Weather forecast is the science and technology used to predict atmospheric conditions at specific times and locations. It has been practiced since ancient times, with Babylonians using natural phenomena for short-term weather predictions.

Hydrometeor

Hydrometeor refers to weather phenomena caused by water in the air, such as dew, fog, rain, and snow. It plays a crucial role in Earth's water cycle and impacts human life and ecosystems worldwide.

Litometer is a weather phenomenon caused by non-airborne particles like dust and smoke, which can affect air quality.
